Chamomile

Matricaria chamomilla

Calming Digestive Anti-inflammatory Gentle

Chamomile is one of the most ancient medicinal herbs known to mankind. This gentle, daisy-like flower has been used for thousands of years to calm nerves, soothe digestive issues, and promote restful sleep.
