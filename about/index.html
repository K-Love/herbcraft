About herbcraft.app

Educational research for a modern world.

herbcraft.app is an educational tool designed to aggregate and organize botanical information from authoritative sources, including peer-reviewed journals, historical texts, and public health databases.

Our goal is radical transparency. We believe that by providing clear citations and safety data, we can empower users to conduct their own independent research into the fascinating world of herbalism.

Note: We do not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
